	once per turn destroys face-up to move pendulum from extra to hand
	once per turn draws card when card in pendulum leaves field

beyond the pendulum
	1x pendulum, 1x effect
	on summon, pay 1200, search pendulum to hand, effect null until pendulum summon
	if pendulum summons under arrows are of different levels, pop 2

	enables fire deploy
	backshift monster in controlled lane

shinra bansho

	vshift

derived rules

beyond and electrumite
	after electrumite, viscount should be in hand and at least one unit should be on field
	during beyond, viscount should be in a pendulum zone and the summoned units can be whatever
	after beyond, viscount should vshift and place a high unit in slots 2 or 4

unit acquisition priority
	priestess
	ninja (if no solo)
	viscount
	marquess (if solo)

vshift priority
	shinra before konig destruction
	ninja before late extra summons
	genesis and arktos
	marquess

hshift priority
	baron before early extra summons
	ninja before late extra summons
	arktos if something still needs to be shifted by then
	genesis or arktos in pendulum if vshift surplus
	solo due to genericness

viscount strings
	pace genesis and arktos with viscount
	viscount genesis for pendulum summoning generally leads to good boards and recovery
	genesis or arktos can be used as link material and returned with viscount and self summon

placements
	keep sidelanes clear in general
	deploying L5+ from spell zones 2 or 4 with viscount and vshift leads to genesis
	keep zones 1 3 5 clean for beyond
	position electrumite and beyond for vshift
	deprioritize threats in opposing monster zones 3 and 5
	during beyond the pendulum, position viscount in 1 or 5 to set up viscount hshift genesis free deploy

	2		4		genesis vshift

priestess openings
	just priestess: 1x vaylantz, priestess hshift unused, solo in gy
	just solo: 1x vaylantz, fields, solo hshift ready
